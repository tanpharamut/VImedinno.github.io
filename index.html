BiTNet

BiTNet is an advanced artificial intelligence system designed to assist medical professionals in analyzing biliary tract ultrasound images. Developed by a team of experts at Khon Kaen University in collaboration with the Cholangiocarcinoma Research Institute, BiTNet aims to improve early detection of cholangiocarcinoma (bile duct cancer) and related conditions.

Key Features.

- AI-powered analysis of ultrasound images
- Assistance for general practitioners and radiologists
- Improved accuracy in detecting 15 classes of abnormalities
- Reduced workload for expert radiologists
- Increased overall diagnostic accuracy by 18%
- Increased general practitioner accuracy by 26%
